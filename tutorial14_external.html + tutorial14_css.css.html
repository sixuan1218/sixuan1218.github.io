TNT

TEENS IN TIMES in short TNT, is a boy group from China which consist ofseven members. Ma JiaQi, Ding ChengXin, Song YaXuan, Liu YaoWen , Zhang ZhenYuan, Yan HaoXiang and He JunLin.

The Group are formed in 2019 under a company called TF Entertainment.

Their group supporting colour is yellow/black and their fans names is called popcorn.
